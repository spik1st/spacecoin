What Is Spacecoin?

Spacecoin is a peer-to-peer Internet currency that enables instant, near-zero cost payments to anyone in the world. Spacecoin is an open source, global payment network that is fully decentralized without any central authorities. Mathematics secures the network and empowers individuals to control their own finances. Spacecoin features faster transaction confirmation times and improved storage efficiency than the leading math-based currency. With substantial industry support, trade volume and liquidity, Spacecoin is a proven medium of commerce complementary to Bitcoin.
